TALK WITH SOMEONE

Our friendships are among the most valuable relationships we have. We gain in various ways from different friendships. We may talk to friends in confidence about things we wouldn't discuss with our families. Or sometimes we just want to talk to someone who will not judge us or just want to make new friends.

Here are few websites for you:

1. Omegle

Omegle is a great way to meet new friends, even while practicing social distancing. When you use Omegle, they pick someone else at random and let you talk one-on-one. To help you stay safe, chats are anonymous unless you tell someone who you are.

2. MOCO

This website also allows you to access chat rooms of your specific interest along with its features of one-to-one and group conversations. This website is known for its authenticity, as it has no fake profiles. So, you can join this website to make some genuine friends on the Internet.

3. Chatroulette

This is a platform with goal to link you to casual people through webcam-based chats.

4. tinychat

This website offers instant messaging and video chatting to allow interaction among its users. In addition to these basic features, Tinychat presents the characteristic of chat rooms, where users can also create their own personal chat rooms to talk on a particular topic or category.

5. MeetMe

This website allows you to chat with the people of your interest and make hundreds of friends. It also enables you to see how many people have liked or adored your profile. This website has a user-base of around 100 million people.
